Seventeen

/ˈsev.ənˌtin/

A group of 13 talented individuals who are known for their self-choreographed routines, youthful energy, and genre-spanning music. Seventeen is often called a "self-producing" idol group because the members are actively involved in the composition, lyrics, and choreography of their songs. Their musical style showcases a fusion of pop, R&B, and EDM, and they are known for their synchronized dance performances. The group has gained international recognition and has a dedicated fanbase called "Carats." Seventeen has released several chart-topping albums and EPs, and their tracks are known for their catchy hooks and meaningful lyrics. The group has also won numerous awards for their music and performances, solidifying their status as multi-talented performers in the K-pop industry.
