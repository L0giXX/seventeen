About Jungkook

Jeon Jung-kook, better known as Jungkook, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is the youngest member of the popular K-pop group BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys.

Jungkook was born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea. He joined BTS in 2013 and quickly gained attention for his impressive dance skills, powerful vocals, and charming personality.
